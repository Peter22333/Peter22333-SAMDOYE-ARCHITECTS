DESIGN PROCESS

From first sketch to built work.

A clear and structured workflow keeps projects grounded in reality while leaving room for discovery.

We assemble each team around the specific needs of the brief. Throughout, you work directly with the architects leading your project.

Our process is transparent: shared timelines, open budgets and regular design reviews.

STEP 01

Consultation & Brief

We listen, visit the site and define ambitions, constraints, budget and schedule together.

STEP 02

Concept Design

We explore options through plans, models and visuals, testing how you will live and work in the space.

STEP 03

Design Development

The preferred concept is refined with structure, materials, detailing and key technical input.

STEP 04

Documentation

We prepare drawings and specifications for permits, tender and construction.

STEP 05

Construction Support

We remain present on site, clarifying details and ensuring the design is delivered as intended.
