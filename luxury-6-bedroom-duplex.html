Luxury 6-Bedroom Duplex Residential Design

Port Harcourt

Client Mr Kabir
Area 1,200sqm
Type Architecture / Museum
Status Completed

The layout features a spacious and well-equipped kitchen, dedicated laundry area, family lounge, and a private study room for work or quiet retreat. A generous ante room enhances the sense of arrival, while an elegant visitors’ room provides comfort and privacy for guests. The overall design emphasizes spacious interiors, functional zoning, and a strong expression of luxury living..
