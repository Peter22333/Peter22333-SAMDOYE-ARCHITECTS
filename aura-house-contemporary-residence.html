Aura House: A Study in Modern Serenity

Ilorin , Nigeria

Client Mr deji
Area 450sqm
Type Residential design
Status Completed

Aura House is a contemporary 3-bedroom residence designed as a private sanctuary from the bustle of modern life. This home prioritizes spatial flow and natural light, featuring a sprawling open-plan kitchen that transitions seamlessly into a voluminous living area. Integrated with a sleek, private garage and surrounded by lush landscaping, the design harmonizes clean architectural lines with the calming influence of its environment. It is a home built for both grand entertaining and quiet reflection..
