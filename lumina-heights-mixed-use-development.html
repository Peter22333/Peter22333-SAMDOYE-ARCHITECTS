Lumina Heights: Mixed-Use Innovation

Gwagwalada Abuja..

Client Proposed
Area 8plots
Type Residential
Status Completed

Lumina Heights is a 9-floor contemporary condominium designed for the next generation of residents. This mixed-use marvel blends boutique commercial spaces with innovative academic zones, creating a space where commerce and education meet. A significant portion of the floor plan is dedicated to recreational health, including a high-performance gym and wellness spa. The residential suites are designed with open-concept layouts, ensuring that every inhabitant experiences the peak of modern comfort within a vibrant, multi-purpose environment..
