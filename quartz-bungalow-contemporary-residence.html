The Quartz is a masterclass in space optimization, offering a full 4-bedroom program on a curated 450sqm footprint. This contemporary bungalow features a fluid, open-plan core that connects the living and dining areas to a sleek kitchen, creating an illusion of vastness. Large floor-to-ceiling windows are strategically placed to pull in natural light, while the private quarters are tucked away to ensure tranquility.
